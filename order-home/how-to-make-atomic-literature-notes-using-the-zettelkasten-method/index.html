Highlights

  • A fleeting note is a temporary, raw capture of what comes to mind or what you have seen (quotes, excerpts, ideas). 2022-05-23
  • The fleeting note can reference the original material, whether it’s a book, article, video, etc. This allows you to trace back to where you originally captured the information in case you find that your understanding wasn’t correct and you need to review the source material again. 2022-05-23
  • A literature note is where you take individual concepts from your fleeting note and extract the core idea of one of those concepts into a single note. It is written in your own words and that shows you have understood of the idea conveyed. You will end up creating many literature notes from a single fleeting note. 2022-05-23
