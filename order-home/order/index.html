Order

A specialized, opinionated, local-first notes app on top of an Obsidian-compatible vault: masonry of cards, in-place WYSIWYG editing, drill-down filtering by Areas and Notable Folders, and list folders that render bullet wikilinks as a drag-reorderable card grid.
