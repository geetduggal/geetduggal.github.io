Tools

Projects I've built and shipped.

  • Kanban Plus · A fork of Obsidian Kanban with cross-file list management and Full Calendar integration for power users who want more from markdown-backed task boards.
  • Full Calendar Plus · A fork of Obsidian Full Calendar adding a linear year view and property-based filtering so you can see your projects as time-spans alongside daily events.
  • Thousand Words · An AI skill that bridges ephemeral daily work (eg. Slack, WhatsApp screenshots) to People, Project, and Spaces notes.
  • Order · A specialized, opinionated, local-first notes app on top of an Obsidian-compatible vault: masonry of cards, in-place WYSIWYG editing, drill-down filtering by Areas and Notable Folders, and list folders that render bullet wikilinks as a drag-reorderable card grid.
